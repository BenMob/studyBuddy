Study Buddy

Welcome

Study Buddy is a tool to help find...study buddies! Universities are typically at least 15,000 to 35,000 students, and specific departments can also be overwhelmingly large. With Study Buddy you can find other students studying the same subject either within your school or from anywhere depending on your preference.

You can login, search for a buddy based on subject, and see other students emails that have made the same search before! Study Buddy will make finding study partners seemless, so go on and find a buddy!
